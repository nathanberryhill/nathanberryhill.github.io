About

Nathan
Berryhill

Nathan Berryhill is a young, pro-business, pro-free market, and pro-liberty Republican who supports the entire Bill of Rights (and not just an amendment or two). He's a graduate from UT-Austin and he's running to represent District 54 in the Texas House of Representatives.

Nathan is running for State Representative not to be something, but to do something. He wants to use his knowledge and experience to create economic growth and jobs for Texans and their families. Nathan is running to bring back common sense policies to Texas. Texas is currently the number one place to be in America with low unemployment and a great economy — Nathan is running to keep it that way.

As a modern college graduate from The University of Texas at Austin, Nathan understands how difficult it can be to manage finances with a full course load and full time work. He recognizes the importance of financial aid, scholarships, and other resources to help families pay the rising costs of college tuition.

As the son of a late US Army soldier, he knows what it's like to have a parent absent in the home for long periods of time. He understands the needs of our men and women in uniform, the impact of death or deployment on their families, and the importance of early counseling and mental health resources.

Nathan is running to apply 21st-Century solutions to issues that Texans face. These include rural broadband internet access, banning taxpayer-funded lobbying, criminal justice reform, and abolishing once-and-for-all Daylight Saving Time in Texas. He will continue to press for property tax relief and fight for government transparency at every level from the city to the State Capitol.

Nathan will fight for a future that works for each and every person, from the smallest to the greatest, from the youngest to the oldest. Nathan believes more young people should be politically engaged, and that's why he's decided to run for office himself.

"I am running for State Representative for You, for Us, for Texas."
